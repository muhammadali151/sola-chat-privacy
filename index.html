Sola Chat – Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

1. Introduction

Sola Chat (“we”, “our”, “app”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and handle your information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

Firebase Authentication

When you sign in using Google or email/password, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Profile photo (Google login only)

Local Storage (App History)

We store your chat history locally on your device. No chat messages are stored on any server.

Advertisements

Our app uses Google AdMob Rewarded Ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers for personalized or non-personalized ads.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To allow you to log in and access your account
  • To show rewarded ads (AdMob)
  • To keep chat history inside your device
  • To improve user experience

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with any third-party, except:

  • Firebase Authentication (for login)
  • Google AdMob (for ads)

5. Children’s Privacy

Our app is not intended for users under 13 years of age.
